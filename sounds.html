13.02.26 | Alien noise from my bathroom

I was just minding my business in my apartment when I started hearing this noise. I began recording and moving about the place to figure out where it was coming from. Turns out it was the sliding glass curtain for the shower. Idk if something was causing it to resonate, but I accidentally bumped my chin on it trying to get close and it stopped. I haven't heard it since.
